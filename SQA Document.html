Introduction

This document provides details of the Software Quality Assurance aspect of the Pong Tracker project. Bugs, fixes, and revision history are documented.


SQA Head: Matt Hengeveld


For this document, versions are in the following format:

  • Final Revision.Major Revision.Minor Revision

    • A minor revision includes small changes to content or formatting changes.
    • A major revision includes major changes to content, as well as major formatting changes.
    • A final revision is a version of the document that has been accepted by the client.
